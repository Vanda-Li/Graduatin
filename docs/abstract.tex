\begin{abstract}
  企业私有文档问答不仅要求回答有效，还要求在文档持续更新、多组织权限约束和多轮交互条件下保持授权一致与内容安全。针对现有检索增强生成系统对文档接入、访问控制和会话安全协同支持不足的问题，本文设计并实现了一套基于检索增强生成的智能问答系统。（1）为避免长任务阻塞在线链路，本文设计了可恢复的文档接入与异步入库机制，使上传确认与后台知识化处理解耦；（2）为使检索边界与访问控制边界保持一致，设计了检索前置权限过滤机制，使授权条件直接作用于候选集生成阶段；（3）为缓解追问中的指代漂移与历史误注入，构建了轮次分类驱动的会话状态控制方法，通过选择性历史注入与工作记忆维护收缩后续检索范围；（4）为控制敏感内容在索引、模型输入与日志阶段的传播风险，设计了分块级隐私治理与分阶段保护策略。

  测试表明，续传机制在大文件场景下完整性通过率为 100\%；权限前置过滤在 30 组跨组织权限矩阵测试中实现零越权；选择性历史策略在 90 轮多轮交互中语义通过率达 77.8\%，优于近轮拼接与无历史基线；在当前测试范围内隐私泄漏率为 0。结果说明，系统能在企业私域场景下兼顾问答有效性、授权一致性与内容安全。
\end{abstract}


\begin{abstract*}
  Enterprise question answering over private documents requires not only effective responses, but also consistent authorization and content safety under continuous document updates, multi-organization access constraints, and multi-turn interactions. To address the limited support of existing retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems for the coordinated handling of document ingestion, access control, and conversational safety, this thesis designs and implements an intelligent question-answering system based on RAG. (1) To prevent long-running tasks from blocking the online path, a recoverable document-ingestion and asynchronous indexing mechanism is designed to decouple upload confirmation from backend knowledge processing. (2) To keep retrieval boundaries consistent with access-control boundaries, a pre-retrieval permission-filtering mechanism is introduced so that authorization constraints take effect directly during candidate generation. (3) To mitigate referential drift and inappropriate history injection in follow-up questions, a turn-classification-driven conversation state control method is proposed, using selective history injection and working-memory maintenance to narrow the scope of subsequent retrieval. (4) To control the propagation risk of sensitive content during indexing, model input, and logging, a chunk-level privacy-governance mechanism with stage-specific protection strategies is designed.

  Experimental results show that the resumable upload mechanism achieves a 100\% integrity pass rate in large-file scenarios; pre-retrieval permission filtering yields zero unauthorized returns in 30 cross-organization permission-matrix tests; the selective-history strategy attains a semantic pass rate of 77.8\% over 90 multi-turn interactions, outperforming both recent-history concatenation and the no-history baseline; and the privacy-governance mechanism records zero leakage within the current test scope. These results indicate that the proposed system can simultaneously support answer effectiveness, authorization consistency, and content safety in enterprise private-domain scenarios.
\end{abstract*}
